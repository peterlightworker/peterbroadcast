01 / About

Since 2009, I've been at the heart of live broadcast — from Serie A in Milan to the Premier League in London, from the Olympics to the NFL. I operate EVS and LSM systems for the world's biggest sports events, delivering the replays and highlights that make live sport unforgettable.


I started out as an Assistant Sound Engineer, drawn in by the energy of live TV, the teams, the tech, and the challenge of pulling off something that looks effortless on screen. By 2010, I was hands-on with EVS, and I haven't looked back since.
